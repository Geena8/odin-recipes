Oatmeal with Greens, Tomatoes, and Egg

Description


Ingredients

  • 1/4 c. quick-cooking steel-cut oats
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/4 c. grape tomatoes, sliced in half
  • 1 scallion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tsp. olive oil
  • 3/4 c. baby spinach
  • Soft-boiled egg, halved

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, bring 3/4 cups of water to a boil.Add oats and pinch of salt and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, in small bowl, toss tomatoes, scallion, oil, and pinch of salt.
  3. Remove oatmeal from heat and fold in spinach to wilt. Transfer to serving bowl, top with tomatoes and egg.
